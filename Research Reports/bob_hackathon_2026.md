## About

A nationwide innovation initiative aimed at fostering solutions to emerging challenges in the banking and financial ecosystem. This edition focuses on the **Cybersecurity & Fraud domain**, inviting students, researchers, startups, developers, fintech innovators, and cybersecurity professionals to build next-generation banking security solutions.

---

## Theme 2026 — Identity Trust, Protection & Safety

Design a **privacy-first, risk-based Identity Trust framework** capable of continuously validating customer and enterprise identities across digital banking channels.

### Problem Areas

- Anomalous user behavior
- New device access attempts
- Suspicious onboarding activities
- Fraudulent account recovery attempts
- Privileged access misuse
- Identity compromise risks

---

## Expected Outcomes

1. Reduce account takeover incidents
2. Prevent KYC and onboarding fraud
3. Detect insider threats and misuse
4. Improve digital trust and customer safety
5. Enable secure and compliant banking access
6. Deliver friction-optimized user experiences
7. Scale across multiple banking channels and high transaction volumes

## Evaluation Criteria

| Criterion | Description |
|---|---|
| Innovation & Originality | Novelty of the proposed solution |
| Technical Feasibility | Practicality and implementation soundness |
